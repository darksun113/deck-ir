# Commercial Licensing

`deck-ir` is offered under a **dual-license** model.

## 1. Open-source license — AGPL-3.0-only

By default, `deck-ir` is licensed under the **[GNU Affero General Public License v3.0](./LICENSE)**.

This is a strong copyleft license. In short, it means:

- You may use, modify, and distribute `deck-ir` freely.
- **Any use, including running it as a network / SaaS service**, triggers the AGPL's obligations: if you distribute or offer to users (over a network) a product that incorporates or is derived from `deck-ir`, you must **make the complete corresponding source of your derivative available under AGPL-3.0**.

If your project is itself open source under AGPL-3.0 (or a compatible license), this option is free and you need nothing further.

## 2. Commercial license

If you want to use `deck-ir` in a **closed-source** product, or as part of a **proprietary SaaS** offering, **without** taking on the AGPL's source-disclosure obligations, you need a **commercial license**.

A commercial license grants you the right to use `deck-ir` in proprietary and closed-source software, free of AGPL copyleft requirements.
